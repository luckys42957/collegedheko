About Courses & Fees Of VIT

VIT Vellore Fees vary depending on the course and specialization opted by the student. VIT Vellore offers undergraduate, postgraduate, integrated, research and industrial programmes in the field of Engineering, Design, Management, Computer Sciences, and many others. Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) is a private university located in Vellore, Tamil Nadu and was established in 1984.

  • VIT Vellore B.Tech Fees is INR 1,73,000 and INR 1,95,000 Per Annum for Group A and Group B respectively.
  • VIT Vellore MBA Fees is INR 3,51,500 for the First Year.
  • The course fee for VIT Vellore UG Courses ranges from INR 57,000 to INR 3,13,000.
  • For PG courses, the fee structure ranges from around INR 60,000 to INR 3,02,000.
  • VIT Vellore Ph.D. Fees is INR 43,000 for full-time candidates.

About Admissions VIT 2024

VIT Vellore Admission to its flagship courses takes place through entrance examinations conducted by the institute itself. Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) University is a private research University located in Vellore, Tamil Nadu. VIT Vellore is ranked 8th among the top Universities in India as per NIRF 2023 report. Moreover, VIT Vellore has been accredited with ‘A++’ grade by NAAC for the last 3 consecutive years. VIT Vellore offers 66 undergraduates, 58 postgraduates, 15 integrated programs, 2 research programs, and 2 M.Tech industrial programs.

  • Admissions to B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA, and Ph.D. are held through VITEEE, VITMEE, VITBEE, and VITREE respectively.
  • Admissions to other courses such as B.Sc (Hons), B.Sc, M.Sc, BCA, BCom, MCA, and MDes are granted on the basis of merit.
  • Every year, VIT Vellore releases 5 cutoff categories for admission to various BTech courses.
  • The first-year fee for the flagship BTech program is INR 1.98 Lakhs.
  • VIT Vellore Ph.D. Fees is INR 43,000 for full-time candidates.

VIT Vellore B.Tech Admission 2024

VIT Vellore offers 4-year B.Tech program in 10 specializations namely, Computer Science & Engineering, Computer Science & Business Systems, Biotechnology, Electrical & Electronics Engineering, Data Sciences, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, etc. The major specializations such as Computer Science, Mechanical and Electronics & Communication include further specializations. VIT Vellore B.Tech Fees is INR 1,73,000 and INR 1,95,000 Per Annum for Group A and Group B respectively. Admission to the VIT Vellore BTech program is based on the VITEEE exam score.
Some major specializations offered for the B.Tech course at VIT Vellore are listed below:

  • Aerospace Engineering
  • Biotechnology
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Computer Science & Engineering
  • Electronics Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering

VIT Vellore B.Tech Eligibility Criteria

Candidates applying for the B.Tech program must check the eligibility criteria set by the authority:
  • Candidate must be a Indian National / NRI/ PIO/ OCI holder.
  • Must have passed 10+2
  • Minimum marks obtained must be 60% in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, or Biology (PCM/ PCB) in class 12 or equivalent exam.
  • Born on or after July 1, 2000.

VIT Vellore B.Tech Selection Criteria

Candidates for admission are selected based on VITEEE marks. Students applying with JEE Main and SAT exam scores are also eligible to apply. The university will shortlist candidates for admission based on cutoff marks set by them. However, VIT Vellore does not release its cutoff list. Examinees in the top merit i.e. from 1 to 20,000 are called to participate in Phase 1 counselling on multiple dates. During the counselling process, the seats will be allocated to the candidates based on their All India Rank and the choices filled by them. VIT Vellore Application Process

VIT Vellore Admisson 2024 is open. VIT Vellore has started accepting applications for VITEEE 2024. Admission at VIT Vellore is based on merit in the university-level examination which includes VITEEE, VITMEE, and VITREE. VIT Vellore also accepts merit in National Level Examinations like CAT, NMAT, CMAT, XAT, etc for MBA courses. VIT Vellore also offers PhD courses through VITREE. The last date to apply for VIT Vellore PhD Admission 2024 is November 30, 2023.
  • Step 1: Visit the official website of VIT Vellore @https://vit.ac.in/.
  • Step 2: Click on the “VITEEE 2024 Apply Now” tab appearing on the top of the home page.The applicant will be redirected to a new window.
  • Step 3: Click on the “New User” tab if not registered already. Fill in the information like Name, DOB, Gender, Email Address, Mobile Number etc.
  • Step 4: Once the registration is successful, a password will be sent from VIT to the registered mobile number and Email ID.
  • Step 5: Login in with your registered email ID and password received from VIT.
  • Step 6: Fill in the application form carefully with all relevant details. An application number will be generated after submission.
  • Step 7: An application fee of INR 1,350 (non-refundable) has to be paid through online mode.
  • Step 8: Upload the necessary documents.
